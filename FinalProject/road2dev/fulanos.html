<Fulano's Game>


<Objective>

The main objective of this game is to remember the importance of separating trash to recycle it. Avoid obstacles and see how many useful objects you can make. Press the green flag to start and the red circle to stop. Use the arrow keys to move Fulano's, good game! Just like in the game, do this in real life and help make the world more sustainable!

<How it was made>

<JHXW - dazelevl.myportfolio.com - Luz da Melodia>

First, I was thinking about making a game with the city of Rio de Janeiro as a backdrop, as it is one of the best-known cities in my country, with Canarinho, another of Brazil's symbols, as its star. However, after meeting one of my brother's characters, the monkey Fulano, I decided to invite him to this project, as an opportunity to show his character and work as a visual designer, so he created the scenery and all the other characters.

<JHXW - dazelevl.myportfolio.com - Visão Ampla>

The reason for making this character the star of this game is his relationship with modern urban life, he is a monkey who makes street art and spreads his thoughts about nature throughout the city through his Graffits. He wants to have a more balanced and sustainable world and to achieve this he will take real action, collecting rubbish and recycling it, returning useful objects resulting from this transformation to society.

<JHXW - dazelevl.myportfolio.com - Da Ponte Rasa Pra Cá>
